UW Study Buddy

Students at UW are provided with many opportunities to connect and meet like-minded students, whether it be through classes or RSOs. What's missing is a digital platform to find like-minded people beyond the means of working on group projects or meeting at club events! This is why we came up with UW Study Buddy, an academic social media platform meant to making finding study groups, making friends in your major, and seeking academic advice or supplies easier!
